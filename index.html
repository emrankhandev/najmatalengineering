Our Company

Najmat Al Engineering Ltd. is a leading high-end manufacturing supporting service enterprise in the field of metal processing in Bangladesh and some metal importing from the world’s largest countries such as: China, Japan, Vietnam, Korea, Canada and USA for local and international market demand fill up.

At present, Najmat Al Engineering has established a number of processing service centers and manufacturing service bases across the country, with customers covering multiple industries. Najmat Al Engineering Ltd is a large-scale Galvanized and Stainless steel, color coating, copper, aluminum, lead production and sales company. It is the first-class agent of many domestic steel mills. The company's production and sales center is located in Dhaka, Chittagong, Khulna, Barisal, Sylhet, Comilla and Rangpur - Bangladesh , with a number of imported processing equipment, which can provide a variety of personalized services according to customer needs.

The company's warehousing center provides 24-hour service and can handle the cargo distribution business for customers. Najmat Al Engineering Ltd. has set up branches in many places in Bangladesh and has started setting up branches in some nearby Indian states which is close to our country’s borders and very soon we are setting up branches in UAE and Africa, with sufficient inventory, which is convenient for local and international customers. Welcome friends from all over the world to come to discuss cooperation!
